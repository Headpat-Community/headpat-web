# Privacy Policy

_Last updated September 13, 2023_

This Privacy Policy for Headpat ("we," "us," or "our") outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you utilize our services ("Services"). This includes interactions with our website at [https://headpat.place](https://headpat.place), our mobile application (Headpat), or any related services that link to this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at [legal@headpat.place](mailto:legal@headpat.place).

## Summary of Key Points

This summary highlights the key aspects of our privacy practices. For detailed information, please refer to the relevant sections of this Privacy Policy.

### What Information Do We Collect?

We collect personal information typically associated with the use of social media platforms. This includes:

- **Communication Data:** Information from communications you engage in on our platform.
- **Gallery Content:** Images and other content you upload to our gallery.
- **Location Data:** Information about your location, if you choose to share it. Location data is only retained while you are actively sharing your location. If you stop sharing your location, all associated data is immediately deleted.

### Age Restrictions

Our Services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. By using our Services, you affirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from someone under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

### Do We Process Sensitive Personal Information?

We do not process sensitive personal information.

### Do We Receive Information from Third Parties?

We do not receive information from third parties.

### How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your information to:

- Provide and enhance our Services
- Communicate with you
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations
- Fulfill any other purposes for which you provide consent

We ensure that any processing of your information is based on valid legal grounds.

### When and With Whom Do We Share Your Personal Information?

We may share your information in specific circumstances and with designated third parties. For details on when and with whom we share your personal information, please see the relevant section below.

### How Do We Protect Your Information?

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information. However, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As such, we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access or breaches.

### What Are Your Privacy Rights?

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights concerning your personal information. These rights can include access, correction, deletion, and restriction of processing. Please refer to the section below for more information on your rights and how to exercise them.

### How Can You Exercise Your Rights?

To exercise your rights, please submit a request via email to [legal@headpat.place](mailto:legal@headpat.place). We will address your request in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

For more comprehensive details on our data practices, please refer to the full Privacy Policy.
